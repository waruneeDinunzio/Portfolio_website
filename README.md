## 👩‍💻 About Me

I began my career managing and owning restaurants, where precision, speed, and customer experience were critical. That mindset now drives how I approach software quality.

While transitioning into tech, I initially pursued software engineering—but through hands-on training and real-world projects, I discovered QA, and it clicked. I enjoy being the “defensive team”: identifying risks, improving systems, and ensuring products truly work for users.

Today, I’m focused on becoming a strong QA Automation Engineer, working with tools like **Playwright, Selenium, and Python**, supported by my ISTQB certification.

I’m especially interested in **ethical AI in testing**, ensuring systems are reliable, fair, and trustworthy.
